About

Rock Paper Scissors is a classic game which has been around for longer than you can imagine!
This website aims to replicate the game through HTML,CSS, and JavaScript.
In single player mode you choose between three options, rock, paper, and scissors
rock beats scissors,scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. With these rules in mind
you can get straight to playing the game now against the server if you wish!
The servers choices are completely random and you are told what you and the server
chose after each turn. Games are played best out of three, with the winner gaining one point on the baord.

Whether you are playing this game for a high score, to settle a decision, or just for enjoyment, good luck, and have fun!
